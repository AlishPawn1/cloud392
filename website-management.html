Website Management

Websites, like many other forms of software, require updates and maintenance to keep them running properly. If you avoid website maintenance, you are potentially putting your website and your brand at great risk – what happens if your website goes down, or worse what happens if it gets hacked?

The website was working fine when it launched, doesn’t mean that will always be the case. Many website owners never check their website to see how it’s functioning. Missed website issues or downtime can lose you, and potential customers.

How it works?

Initial Website Evaluation & Call

We’ll start the process off by completing a comprehensive evaluation and website audit. This helps us identify the biggest opportunities for your website. After we’ve completed this – we’ll jump on a call with you to discuss our findings and next steps.

First Month

In the first month, we’ll create staging site, fixing critical issues and getting it up to date so that we can start to work through the action items we discussed as part of our evaluation.

On-going Support

After the first month, we’ll start to work through any action items within our allocated support. We’ll also be delivering on your monthly website management package inclusions. And if you need something done – we are at your disposal!

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my website goes down or is hacked?

All our Website Maintenance plans include Emergency downtime support, what this entails is us restoring the website to a previous backup. The idea behind this is to get your website back up and running as quickly as possible

Do my monthly hours & tasks roll over?

Yes, we operate on a fair-use policy so that we can deliver a high level of service to all our clients. It will be rolled over next month only.

What if I need something that isn’t covered by my Website Maintenance plan?

We can usually help with this! We’ll be able to quote you, and get your approval for the quote prior to going ahead with any additional work required.
